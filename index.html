Quantized-Light CNNs

This was my Master of Engineering Thesis, as well as the inspiration for my 2 publications. In this project i tried to explore the ways and the optimizations from quantization to hardware level tweaking, in Convolutional Neural Networks, so they can be easily deployed on IOT/Mobile/Edge Computing Devices. Quantization and Pruning led to implementations of 16/8/4 floating point representations, with little to no accuracy loss in datasets like MNIST/CIFAR10/IMAGENET. Below you can find the link for my Vivado(VHDL based RTL implementation) repo of the first paper.
